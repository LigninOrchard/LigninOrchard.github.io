John Smith was born sometime in the mid to late 1700s. He died most likely in 1846 in York County, NB.
He had several children with his wife Mary, including Lavinia, William Sidney, John (Jr.), Mary, and Emily Ann.


Lavinia Smith was born about 1801 in NB. She married William Crane in about 1823.


William Sidney Smith was born around 1806 in Kingsclear, York, NB. He married Jane Hitchings in 1830 in St. Andrew's, Charlotte, NB.


Mary Smith was born around 1807 in Kingsclear, York, NB. She married William Finnemore in 1823. She later married Michael Lavine.


John Smith (Jr.) was born about 1811 and did not marry. He died in about 1881.
